# 02 · Design Language: LinkedIn Drag, Hinge Bones

## Palette

- **Primary:** LinkedIn blue `#0A66C2` on white
- **App background:** `#F3F2EF` (LinkedIn's exact feed gray)
- **Headers:** navy
- **Open-to-Work badge states:** green / purple / grey

The app should look like a B2B SaaS tool that is inexplicably a dating app —
that deadpan is the joke.

## Typography & motifs

Clean corporate sans (Inter); letterhead, memo, and org-chart motifs; the
match celebration is a formal **Offer Letter** document; profiles render with
resume typography (section rules, bullet points).

## Corporate cartoons (free AND part of the satire)

Lean into "Corporate Memphis" — the flat, big-limbed big-tech illustration
style:

- **unDraw** — free, accent color settable to `#0A66C2`
- **Open Peeps / Humaaans** — free/CC0 mix-and-match people illustrations

Use for empty states, onboarding, paywalls. AI-generate custom scenes in the
same style for hero moments (offer letter art, rejection letter art).

## Dress code (the differentiator)

This is a corporate dating app — no shirtless mirror selfies. Photo slots are
labeled and guided:

1. **Professional Headshot** (required first photo — crop guides and lighting
   tips in the upload flow)
2. **Business Casual**
3. **At the Office / In the Field**
4. **Casual Friday** (the one relaxed slot)

Onboarding copy: *"Dress for the date you want, not the date you have."*
Community guidelines frame it as the office dress code; reports include a
"violates dress code" reason. Enforced culturally (guidelines + reporting +
moderation), not by AI, at launch.

## Satire baked into content, not just chrome

- Optional **Department** archetype badge on profiles: Finance Bro, Tech Bro,
  Neuroscience Girlie, Consultant (Will Explain Your Own Job To You), Founder
  Era, Med Student (Unavailable Until 2031), Gym Is My Second Office, …
- Hustle-culture Behavioral Questions ("My toxic trait is checking Slack on
  vacation," "My 5am morning routine includes…")
- Archetypes are filterable (free) and endlessly extensible for content
  marketing.
